Our story

Pest problems, solved right the first time

We started Pasternack Pest Management because we believe pest control should be done with care and craftsmanship — not rushed with a quick spray and a goodbye.

Meet the founder

Why Erik started PPM

Erik Pasternack founded PPM in 2022 to make one goal a reality: make pests a thing of the past.

After working for several other pest control companies, Erik grew frustrated with the lack of time and care put into actually solving people's pest problems. While others "spray and pray," PPM takes a holistic approach — inspecting and analyzing each home to build a custom solution. Some jobs call for spraying, some for patching holes, and many for both. Whatever the right answer is, we find it.

We don't rush our jobs, because quality solutions take time — and our work speaks for itself.
