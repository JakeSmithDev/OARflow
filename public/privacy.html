Privacy Policy

We collect only the information needed to answer requests, schedule service, communicate clearly, and process payment securely.

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Information We Collect

When you request a quote, book service, call, text, email, or pay an invoice, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, service address, pest concern, appointment details, and payment status.

How We Use It

We use your information to respond to your request, provide pest control service, send appointment and billing messages, maintain customer records, improve our site, and meet business or legal obligations.

Payments

Card payments and saved payment methods are handled by our payment processor. We do not store full card numbers on this website.

Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand page visits and phone-call clicks. Analytics are configured without selling personal information.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us run the business, such as scheduling, payment, messaging, email, hosting, or analytics providers.

Your Choices

You can contact us to update your information or ask questions about this policy. Some records may need to be retained for billing, safety, legal, or operational reasons.
