What we treat

Pests we handle

A closer look at the most common pests we treat across Maryland homes and businesses.

Health risk

Cockroaches

Cockroaches spread bacteria, trigger allergies and asthma, and contaminate food. They breed quickly and are tough to eliminate once established. We treat the infestation at its source and seal harborage areas so they don't return.

Health & property

Rats & Mice

Rodents gnaw wiring, contaminate food, and carry disease. Beyond removal, our exclusion work seals the gaps they use to get in — the step most companies skip — so the problem stays solved.

Sting hazard

Stinging Insects

Wasps and hornets become aggressive when their nest is threatened and can sting repeatedly — a serious risk for anyone with allergies. Let us remove nests safely instead of attempting it yourself.

Nuisance & property

Ants

Ants contaminate food, attract other pests, and some species damage structures by nesting in walls. We trace trails back to the colony and treat at the source rather than just the ants you see.

Some venomous

Spiders

Most spiders are harmless, but black widows and brown recluses can be dangerous — especially for children and the elderly. We clear webs, treat hiding spots, and reduce the insects they feed on.

Disease vector

Mosquitoes

Mosquitoes are more than a nuisance — they transmit disease. We treat the areas where larvae breed to stop them at the source, and where adults rest, to cut down the population on your property.

Spreads fast

Bed Bugs

Bed bugs feed at night and hide in bedding and furniture. Their bites cause itching and discomfort, and infestations spread quickly. Contact us for a thorough inspection and treatment plan.

Property damage

Termites

Termites feed on the wood inside your walls and can cause costly structural damage before you ever see them. Regular inspection is key — if you suspect activity, contact us right away.
